Lab 5

When designing a webpage, it is important to follow best practices to ensure accessibility, usability, and responsiveness. A structured layout with clear navigation improves the users experience by making content easy to find and interact with. You can make use of page layout properties like flexbox, grid, and float to adjust the layout of your webpage to make it not only visualy appealing but also user friendly. You can use one or many media query to adjust different properties based on desired conditions to make your webpage accessible and usuable without compromising the look for all devices.
