Observations

An observation or lessons learned by testing on multiple browsers and devices that I learned is that less is more. throught this project I was constantly reminded that keeping is simple is best. Adding more whether it be adding more styling or information it's best to keep it simple without compromising visual appeal. If there is to much going on your more likley to confuse youself and just cause a big mess. It's best to keep your website simple and if you want to add more you can always add after you have your foundation down. While testing I verified that it was compatible with safari, chrome, firefox and that it was compatible with both my laptop and iphone. This whole process reinforced the importance of simplicity because more complex features behaved diffrently in other browsers but a simple layout ensured that my page would look how it is intened on every browser across any device.
